Who is Daniel Gao

Daniel Gao is a Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultant (RCIC) and an active member of the College of Immigration and Citizenship Consultants (CICC). After migrating to Canada in 2019, he received a Certificate in Immigration Laws, Policies and Procedures Program from the University of British Columbia in 2021.

Daniel is experienced in Canadian immigration consulting and global residence and citizenship planning. He has spent fifteen years as a successful Case Specialist, Project Manager and Department Manager at a leading immigration consulting firm in Beijing, China. During this time Daniel has helped more than 1,000 families who chose Canada, Europe or Caribbean destinations to begin their new lives.

Located in Halifax, Nova Scotia, Gao Immigration Consulting is well positioned to assist you and your family to become future Canadians by matching you with one of the more than 100 available Canadian immigration programs.

Daniel is eager to serve all of your immigration needs.
